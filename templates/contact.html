Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PDFy

How do I compress a PDF file? ‚ûï

To compress a PDF file, simply visit our website, select the "Compress PDF" tool, upload your file, and choose your desired compression level. Our system will process your file and provide a download link for the compressed version.

Is PDFy completely free to use? ‚ûï

Yes, all our PDF tools are completely free to use with no hidden costs. We don't require registration, and there are no watermarks on your processed documents.

How secure are my documents? ‚ûï

We take security seriously. All file transfers are encrypted with SSL, and your documents are automatically deleted from our servers after processing. We never share your files with third parties.

What file formats do you support? ‚ûï

We support all common document formats including PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX, XLS, XLSX, JPG, PNG, and more. Our conversion tools allow you to convert between these formats easily.

How long does processing take? ‚ûï

Processing time depends on file size and the specific tool you're using. Most documents are processed within seconds. For very large files (over 100MB), processing may take up to a minute.
