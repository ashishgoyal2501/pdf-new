Our Journey to PDF Excellence

PDFy was founded in 2018 by a team of document management experts frustrated with the complexity and cost of existing PDF solutions. We believed powerful PDF tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise or budget.

Starting from a small office with just three developers, we've grown into a global platform serving millions of users each month. Our mission remains clear: to remove friction from PDF management and make document workflows effortless.

What began as a simple PDF compression tool has evolved into a comprehensive suite of document solutions. Today, we offer 12 different PDF tools that handle everything from editing and conversion to security and optimization - all completely free and accessible right in your browser.

As we continue to grow, our commitment remains: to provide the most intuitive, secure, and powerful PDF tools without compromising on quality or accessibility.
